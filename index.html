Pick decks to grow your money.

You start with $2000. Each turn, you pick one of four decks. Every pick wins you some money. Some picks also make you pay a fee.

After you pick a deck, tap Collect to update your money and move on. You can use touch, a mouse, or the number keys 1 to 4.

  1. Pick one deck: A, B, C, or D.
  2. See how much you won and any fee you have to pay.
  3. Tap Collect, then keep going until you finish all 100 picks.
You can't see how each deck works ahead of time. The point is to figure out the best decks as you play.

Game info

This game runs right in your web browser. Nothing is saved unless you download your results when you finish.
